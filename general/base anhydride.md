# base anhydride

__Base anhydrides__ are {{[oxides](oxide.md) of [group 1](group%20(periodic%20table).md#^group-1) or [group 2](alkaline%20earth%20metal.md) [elements](chemical%20element.md)}}. They are obtained by {{[dehydration](dehydration%20reaction.md) of the corresponding [hydroxide](hydroxide.md) [bases](base%20(chemistry).md)}}. They can be [rehydrated](hydration%20reaction.md) {{to reobtain the corresponding hydroxide [salts](salt%20(chemistry).md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-11-07,154,290!2024-12-10,437,290!2025-06-28,612,330-->
