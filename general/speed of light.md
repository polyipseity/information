# speed of light

The __speed of light__ {{in vacuum}}, commonly denoted ___c___, is {{a universal [physical constant](physical%20constant.md)}} that is exactly equal to {{299&nbsp;792&nbsp;458 metres per second (approximately 300&nbsp;000 kilometres per second}}; 186&nbsp;000 miles per second; 671 million miles per hour).
