# work (physics)

## mathematical calculation

> __infinitesimal linear work__
>
> {{$$\delta W = \vec{F} \cdot \,\mathrm{d}\vec{s} = \vec{F} \cdot \vec{v} \,\mathrm{d}t$$}}
>
> - where
>   - {{$\delta W$ is the infinitesimal __work__ done by a [force](force.md) $\vec{F}$ over an infinitesimal [displacement (geometry)](displacement%20(geometry).md) $\mathrm{d}\vec{s}$ or at an instantaneous [velocity](velocity.md) $\vec{v}$ over an infinitesimal [time](time.md) $\mathrm{d}t$}} <!--SR:!2024-09-28,218,330!2024-10-31,243,330-->

<!-- markdownlint MD028 -->

> __infinitesimal angular work__
>
> {{$$\delta W = \vec\tau \cdot \,\mathrm{d}\vec\theta = \vec\tau \cdot \vec\omega \,\mathrm{d}t$$}}
>
> - where
>   - {{$\delta W$ is the infinitesimal __work__ done by a [torque](torque.md) $\vec\tau$ over an infinitesimal [angular displacement](angular%20displacement.md) $\mathrm{d}\vec\theta$ or at an instantaneous [angular velocity](angular%20velocity.md) $\vec\omega$ over an infinitesimal [time](time.md) $\mathrm{d}t$}} <!--SR:!2024-10-04,222,330!2024-04-15,82,290-->
