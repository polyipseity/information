# neutrino detector

A __neutrino detector__ is {{a physics apparatus which is designed to study [neutrinos](neutrino.md)}}. Because neutrinos only [weakly interact](weak%20interaction.md) with other particles of matter, {{neutrino detectors must be very large to detect a significant number of neutrinos}}. Neutrino detectors are often built {{underground, to isolate the detector from [cosmic rays](cosmic%20ray.md) and other background radiation}}. <!--SR:!2024-07-25,4,270!2024-07-25,4,270!2024-07-25,4,270-->
