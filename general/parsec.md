# parsec

The __parsec__ (symbol: __pc__) is {{a [non-SI](International%20System%20of%20Units.md) [unit of length](unit%20of%20length.md) defined as the distance at which 1 [astronomical unit](astronomical%20unit.md) (au) [subtends](subtended%20angle.md) an [angle](angle.md) of one [arcsecond](minute%20and%20second%20of%20arc.md) ($\frac1{3600}$ of a [degree](degree%20(angle).md)), i.e. $1\ \text{pc}=\frac{1\ \text{au} }{\tan1''}$, corresponding to $\frac{648000}\pi$ au}}.
