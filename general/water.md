# water

__Water__ is {{an [inorganic compound](inorganic%20compound.md) with the [chemical formula](chemical%20formula.md) __H<sub>2</sub>O__}}. In its [solid](solid.md) state, {{it is [ice](ice.md)}}. <!--SR:!2026-11-23,1016,330!2024-04-18,291,330-->

Ice has {{at least 18 [phases](phase%20(matter).md)}}. The most common on [Earth](Earth.md) is {{[ice I<sub>h</sub>](ice%20Ih.md)}}. <!--SR:!2025-04-20,555,310!2024-05-31,329,330-->
