# Lagoon Nebula

The {{__Lagoon Nebula__}} (catalogued as {{__Messier 8__ or __M8__, __NGC 6523__, __Sharpless 25__, __RCW 146__, and __Gum 72__}}) is {{a giant [interstellar cloud](interstellar%20cloud.md) in the [constellation](constellation.md) [Sagittarius](Sagittarius%20(constellation).md)}}. It is classified as {{an [emission nebula](emission%20nebula.md) and has an [H II region](H%20II%20region.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-08-05,2,250!2024-08-06,2,190!2024-08-05,2,230!2024-08-05,1,170-->
