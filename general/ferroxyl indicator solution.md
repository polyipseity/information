# ferroxyl indicator solution

__Ferroxyl indicator solution__ {{consists of [potassium hexacyanoferrate(III)](potassium%20ferricyanide.md) and [phenolphthalein](phenolphthalein.md)}}. The former turns {{blue in the presence of [iron](iron.md)(II) ions}}. The latter turns {{pink in the presence of [hydroxide ions](hydroxide.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-04-07,87,230!2024-06-29,325,290!2024-02-13,239,310-->
