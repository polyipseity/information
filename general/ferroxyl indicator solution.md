# ferroxyl indicator solution

__Ferroxyl indicator solution__ {{consists of [potassium hexacyanoferrate(III)](potassium%20ferricyanide.md) and [phenolphthalein](phenolphthalein.md)}}. The former turns {{blue in the presence of [iron](iron.md)(II) ions}}.
