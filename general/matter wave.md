# matter wave

__Matter waves__ {{describe the [wave](wave.md)-like behavior of all [matter](matter.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-02-20,216,310-->

## de Broglie relations

The _de Broglie wavelength_ $\lambda$ {{relates to the [momentum](momentum.md) $p$ and the [frequency](frequency.md) $f$ relates to the total [energy](energy.md) $E$ of a [free particle](free%20particle.md)}}: <!--SR:!2025-03-23,454,290-->

> The de Broglie equations:
>
> - {{$\lambda=\frac{h}{p}$}}
> - {{$f=\frac{E}h$}}
>
> where {{$h$ is the [Planck constant](Planck%20constant.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-07-13,274,270!2025-07-30,541,290!2025-02-21,504,330-->
