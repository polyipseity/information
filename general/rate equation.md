# rate equation

The __rate equation__ of a [chemical reaction](chemical%20reaction.md) {{is an [equation](equation.md) that shows the quantitative relationship between the [reaction rate](reaction%20rate.md) and the [concentration](concentration.md) of [reactants](reagent.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-08-13,26,230-->
