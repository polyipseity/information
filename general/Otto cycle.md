## cycle analysis

> __maximum thermal efficiency of a Otto cycle__
>
> {{$$\eta_\mathrm{th} = 1 - \frac1{r^{\gamma - 1} }$$}}
>
> - where
>     - {{$\gamma$ is the [heat capacity ratio](heat%20capacity%20ratio.md) and $r = \frac{V_1}{V_2}$ is the [compression ratio](compression%20ratio.md) (i.e. the ratio of the [volume](volume.md) at its maximum over that at its minimum)}} <!--SR:!2024-01-04,13,270!2024-01-07,14,290-->
