# isotope

__Isotopes__ are {{distinct [nuclides](nuclide.md) of the same [element](chemical%20element.md)}}. They have {{the same [atomic number](atomic%20number.md) but different [mass numbers](mass%20number.md)}}. Isotopes of an element have {{almost the same [chemical properties](chemical%20property.md) but different [physical properties](physical%20property.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-03-16,229,270!2024-03-29,278,330!2024-05-07,99,250-->
