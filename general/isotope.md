# isotope

__Isotopes__ are {{distinct [nuclides](nuclide.md) of the same [element](chemical%20element.md)}}. They have {{the same [atomic number](atomic%20number.md) but different [mass numbers](mass%20number.md)}}. Isotopes of an element have {{almost the same [chemical properties](chemical%20property.md) but different [physical properties](physical%20property.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-07-29,82,270!2023-06-23,63,310!2023-07-09,69,270-->
