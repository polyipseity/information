# panning

__Panning__ is {{the extraction of [mineral](mineral.md) from a [placer deposit](placer%20deposit.md) using a pan}}.

It can extract {{unreactive [elements](element.md) like [gold](gold.md) and [platinum](platinum.md)}}.
