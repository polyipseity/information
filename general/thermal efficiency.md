# thermal efficiency

## heat engines

> __thermal efficiency of a heat engine__
>
> {{$$\eta_\mathrm{th} \equiv \frac{W}{Q_\mathrm{in}} = \frac{Q_\mathrm{in} - \lvert Q_\mathrm{out} \rvert}{Q_\mathrm{in}} = 1 - \frac{\lvert Q_\mathrm{out} \rvert}{Q_\mathrm{in}}$$}}
>
> - where
>     - {{$W$ is the net [work](work%20(physics).md) done by the system, and $Q_\mathrm{in}$ and $Q_\mathrm{out}$ are respectively the [heat](heat.md) supplied to and given off by the system}} <!--SR:!2023-12-22,4,270!2023-12-22,4,270-->
