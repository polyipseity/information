# Pareto principle

The __Pareto principle__, also known as {{the __80/20 rule__, the __law of the vital few__, and the __principle of factor sparsity__}} states that {{for many events, about 80% of consequences come from 20% of the causes}}. <!--SR:!2024-06-08,4,270!2024-06-08,4,270-->
