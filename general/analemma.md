# analemma

In [astronomy](astronomy.md), an __analemma__ ({{/ˌænəˈlɛmə/}}; from {{[Ancient Greek](Ancient%20Greek.md) ἀνάλημμα (analēmma) 'support'}}) is {{a [diagram](diagram.md) showing the [position of the Sun](position%20of%20the%20Sun.md) in the sky as seen from a fixed location on [Earth](Earth.md) at the same [mean solar time](solar%20time.md#mean%20solar%20time)}}, as that position {{varies over the course of a year}}. The diagram will resemble {{a [figure eight](lemniscate.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-07-09,7,250!2024-07-07,5,230!2024-07-03,4,270!2024-07-03,4,270!2024-07-03,4,270-->
