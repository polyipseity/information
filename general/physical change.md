# physical change

__Physical changes__ are {{changes affecting the form of [chemical substances](chemical%20substance.md)}}. They contrast with [chemical changes](chemical%20change.md). <!--SR:!2023-11-28,133,250-->

In a physical change, substances {{undergo a change without affecting their [chemical composition](chemical%20composition.md)}}. No {{new substances are formed}}. <!--SR:!2023-10-30,127,250!2024-05-21,319,330-->
