# tension

## effects

Under {{uniaxial tension}}, ordinary material are likely to undergo {{elastic deformation}}; that is, {{elongating or necking, returning to its original length and shape once the tension is removed}}. <!--SR:!2024-05-31,11,270!2024-07-02,34,290!2024-07-06,37,290-->
