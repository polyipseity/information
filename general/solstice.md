# solstice

A __solstice__ is {@{the time when the [Sun](Sun.md) reaches its most northerly or southerly [excursion](sun%20path.md) relative to the [celestial equator](celestial%20equator.md) on the [celestial sphere](celestial%20sphere.md)}@}. This occurs {@{twice each year, around June 20–22 ([June solstice](June%20solstice.md)) and December 20–22 ([December solstice](December%20solstice.md))}@}. <!--SR:!2025-06-22,272,330!2024-11-28,96,290-->
