> 4. [Oxyanions](oxyanion.md) are named in the form of _\*-ate_ (group number), _\*-ite_ (group number - 2), or _hypo-\*-ite_ (group number - 4). ### molecular compounds

{{Inorganic}} molecular compounds are {{named with an [IUPAC numerical multiplier](IUPAC%20numerical%20multiplier.md#data) prefix before each [element](chemical%20element.md) and an _-ide_ suffix after the last element}}. The elements are {{ordered in ascending [electronegativity](electronegativity.md)}}. Additionally, {{_mono-_ is not used with the first element}}. Exceptions exist. <!--SR:!2024-06-14,343,337!2025-04-07,446,257!2025-08-18,654,317!2025-05-06,559,317-->
