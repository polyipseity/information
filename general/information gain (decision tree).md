# information gain

## general definition

The information gain of an [attribute](feature%20(machine%20learning).md) $a$ on a set $T$ is defined as {{$$\operatorname{IG}(T, a) = \Eta(T) - \Eta(T | a)$$, where $\Eta(T)$ is the [entropy](entropy%20(information%20theory).md) of $T$ and $\Eta(T | a)$ is the [conditional entropy](conditional%20entropy.md) of $T$ given $a$}}. <!--SR:!2024-04-16,4,270-->
