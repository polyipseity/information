# exponential integral

## definitions

For nonzero [real](real%20number.md) $x$, $Ei(x)$ is defined as:

> {{__for nonzero [reals](real%20number.md)__}}
>
> {{$\operatorname{Ei}(x)=-\int_{-x}^\infty\!\frac{e^{-t} }t\,\mathrm{d}t=\int_{-\infty}^x\!\frac{e^t}t\,\mathrm{d}t\qquad{}x\in\mathbb{R}_{\ne0}$}} <!--SR:!2023-11-01,26,290!2023-10-12,6,230-->
