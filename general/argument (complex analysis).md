# argument (complex analysis)

## definition

> __definition of arg(_z_)__
>
> 1. {{Geometrically, $\arg z$ is the polar angle $\varphi$ measured counterclockwise in [radians](radian.md) from the positive [real](real%20number.md) axis to the vector representing the [complex number](complex%20number.md) $z$ in the [complex plane](complex%20plane.md).}}
> 2. {{Algebraically, any [real number](real%20number.md) $\varphi$ such that $z = \lvert z \rvert (\cos \varphi + i \sin \varphi) = \lvert z \rvert e^{i \varphi}$.}} <!--SR:!2023-12-21,4,270!2023-12-21,4,270-->

## principal value

The _[principal value](principal%20value.md)_ of $\arg z$ {{is the value in the open-closed [interval](interval%20(mathematics).md) $(-\pi \mathrm{\ rad},\pi \mathrm{\ rad}]$, sometimes denoted $\operatorname{Arg} z$}}. <!--SR:!2023-12-21,4,270-->
