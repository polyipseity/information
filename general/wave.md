### waves on strings

>
> {{$$\frac 1 c = v = \sqrt{\frac{T}{\mu}}$$}}
>
> where
> - {{string properties: $T$ is [tension](tension%20(mechanics).md) and $\mu$ is linear [density](density.md), i.e. mass per unit length}}
