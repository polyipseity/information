An __elastic modulus__ (also known as {{__modulus of elasticity__}}) is {{a measure of an object's resistance to being deformed elastically when [stress](stress%20(mechanics).md) is applied}}. <!--SR:!2024-12-12,156,310!2024-08-19,73,310-->

## definition

An elastic modulus has the form: {{$$\delta \overset {\text{def} } = \frac {\text{stress} } {\text{strain} }$$}}. <!--SR:!2024-08-08,63,310-->
