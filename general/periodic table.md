# periodic table

The __periodic table__ is {{a table arrangement of the [chemical elements](chemical%20element.md)}}. The elements are placed in {{the order of ascending [atomic number](atomic%20number.md)}}. The rows are called {{[periods](period%20(periodic%20table).md)}} and the columns are called {{[groups](group%20(periodic%20table).md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-01-15,218,310!2026-07-25,926,330!2024-03-12,267,330!2024-05-09,312,330-->
