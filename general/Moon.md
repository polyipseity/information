# Moon

The __Moon__ is {{[Earth](Earth.md)'s only [natural satellite](natural%20satellite.md)}}. It [orbits](orbit%20of%20th%20Moon.md) at an [average distance](lunar%20distancee.md) of {{384&nbsp;400 km (238&nbsp;900 mi), about 30 times the diameter of Earth}}. [Tidal forces](tidal%20forces.md) {{between Earth and the Moon have over time [synchronized](tidal%20locking.md) the Moon's orbital period ([lunar month](lunar%20month.md)) with its rotation period ([lunar day](lunar%20day.md)) at 29.5 Earth days}}, causing {{the [same side](near%20side%20of%20the%20Moon.md) of the Moon to always face Earth}}.
