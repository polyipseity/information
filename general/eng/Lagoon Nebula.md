# Lagoon Nebula

The {@{__Lagoon Nebula__}@} (catalogued as {@{__Messier 8__ or __M8__, __NGC 6523__, __Sharpless 25__, __RCW 146__, and __Gum 72__}@}) is {@{a giant [interstellar cloud](interstellar%20cloud.md) in the [constellation](constellation.md) [Sagittarius](Sagittarius%20(constellation).md)}@}. It is classified as {@{an [emission nebula](emission%20nebula.md) and has an [H II region](H%20II%20region.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2025-03-22,153,270!2025-03-03,20,130!2025-03-08,84,210!2025-03-05,104,190-->
