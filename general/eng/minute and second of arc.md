# minute and second of arc

A __minute of arc__, __arcminute__ (__arcmin__), __arc minute__, or __minute arc__, denoted by {@{the symbol `′`}@}, is {@{a unit of [angular](angle.md) measurement equal to 1/60 of one [degree](degree%20(angle).md)}@}. Since one degree is {@{1/360 of a turn, or complete rotation, one arcminute is 1/21600 of a turn}@}. <!--SR:!2027-06-16,822,330!2025-04-27,232,332!2025-05-03,236,332-->

A __second of arc__, __arcsecond__ (__arcsec__), or __arc second__, denoted by {@{the symbol `″`}@}, is {@{1/60 of an arcminute}@}, {@{1/3600 of a degree, 1/1296000 of a turn, and π/648000 (about 1/206264.8) of a [radian](radian.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2025-05-09,241,332!2025-04-23,210,312!2025-08-09,262,272-->
