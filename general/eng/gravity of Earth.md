# gravity of Earth

The __gravity of Earth__, denoted by {@{___g___}@}, is {@{the [net](net%20force.md) [acceleration](acceleration.md) that is imparted to objects due to the combined effect of [gravitation](gravity.md) (from [mass distribution](Mass%20distribution.md) within [Earth](Earth.md)) and the [centrifugal force](centrifugal%20force.md) (from the [Earth's rotation](Earth's%20rotation.md))}@}. <!--SR:!2025-06-05,248,330!2026-07-28,555,310-->
