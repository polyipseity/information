# scattering

__Scattering__ describes {@{a wide range of physical processes where moving [particle](particle.md) or [radiation](radiation.md) deviates from a straight [trajectory](trajectory.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2028-05-22,1187,250-->

## experiments

Scattering experiments are important in {@{discovering new particles}@} and {@{studying structure of particles}@}. Examples include {@{the [Geiger–Marsden experiments](Geiger–Marsden%20experiments.md)}@}. {@{[Laws of physics](laws%20of%20physics.md)}@} are employed in analysis. <!--SR:!2028-07-29,1530,310!2028-04-28,1462,310!fsrs,2034-09-22T02:13:49.021Z,3022,3022.31735044,1.98030797,2,11,0,0,2026-06-14T02:13:49.021Z!2033-09-14,2967,332-->
