# solar prominence

In [solar physics](solar%20physics.md), a __prominence__, sometimes referred to as a __filament__, is {@{a large [plasma](plasma%20(physics).md) and [magnetic field](magnetic%20field.md) structure extending outward from the [Sun](Sun.md)'s surface, often in a loop shape}@}.
