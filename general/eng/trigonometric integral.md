# trigonometric integral

## sine integral

> {@{__Si(x), si(x)__}@}
>
> - {@{$\operatorname{Si}(x)=\int _0 ^x \! \frac {\sin t} t \,\mathrm{d}t$}@}
> - {@{$\operatorname{si}(x)=\int _\infty ^x \!\frac{\sin{t} }t\,\mathrm{d}t=\operatorname{Si}(x)-\frac\pi2$}@} <!--SR:!2029-05-16,1470,330!2026-03-16,644,290!2026-10-25,350,170-->

## cosine integral

> {@{__Cin(x), Ci(x)__}@}
>
> - {@{$\operatorname{Cin}(x)=\int _0 ^x \! \frac {1 - \cos t} t \,\mathrm{d}t$}@}
> - {@{$\operatorname{Ci}(x)=\int _\infty ^x \! \frac{\cos t} t \,\mathrm{d}t = \gamma + \ln x - \operatorname{Cin}(x)$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$\gamma$}@} is {@{the [Euler's constant](Euler's%20constant.md)}@} <!--SR:!2027-11-05,1025,310!2026-11-16,631,230!2025-12-28,111,130!2025-11-11,3,267!2025-11-11,3,267-->
