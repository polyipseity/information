# thermodynamic beta

> __thermodynamic beta__
>
> {@{$$\beta = \frac{1}{k_\text{B} T}$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$\beta$}@} is {@{the __thermodynamic beta__ of a system at [thermodynamic temperature](thermodynamic%20temperature.md) $T$}@}
> - {@{$k_\text{B} = 1.380\,649 \times 10^{-23} \mathrm{\ J \cdot K^{-1} }$}@} is {@{the [Boltzmann constant](Boltzmann%20constant.md)}@} <!--SR:!2029-12-23,1673,330!2026-11-30,823,330!2026-10-07,645,270!2026-02-14,69,328!2025-12-08,17,308-->
