# June solstice

The __June solstice__ or {@{__northern solstice__}@} is {@{the [solstice](solstice.md) on the [Earth](Earth.md) when the [subsolar point](subsolar%20point.md) is at its [northernmost](north.md) point}@}. It is also known as {@{the [summer solstice](summer%20solstice.md) in the Northern Hemisphere and as the [winter solstice](winter%20solstice.md) in the Southern Hemisphere}@}. The [Sun](Sun.md) is directly over {@{the [Tropic of Cancer](Tropic%20of%20Cancer.md) in the Northern Hemisphere}@}. <!--SR:!2030-08-18,1702,330!2027-12-05,927,330!2029-05-19,1381,350!2028-01-08,961,330-->

On {@{the [Gregorian calendar](Gregorian%20calendar.md)}@}, the June solstice can occur {@{as early as June 20 and as late as June 22 in terms of [Universal Time](Universal%20Time.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2027-04-05,755,330!fsrs,2031-11-21T00:00:00.000Z,1950,1949.97677109,1,2,10,0,0,2026-07-20T00:00:00.000Z-->
