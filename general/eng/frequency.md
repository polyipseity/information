# frequency

__Frequency__ (symbol {@{_f_}@}), most often measured in {@{[_hertz_](hertz.md) (symbol: Hz)}@}, is {@{the number of occurrences of a repeating event per [unit of time](unit%20of%20time.md)}@}.
