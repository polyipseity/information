# lead(II) hydroxide

__Lead(II) hydroxide__ is {@{an [inorganic compound](inorganic%20compound.md) with the [chemical formula](chemical%20formula.md) [Pb](lead.md)([OH](hydroxide.md))<sub>2</sub>}@}. It looks like {@{white amorphous [powder](powder.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2026-10-03,995,350!2028-08-13,1128,230-->

## preparation

Treating {@{lead(II) [salts](salt%20(chemistry).md) with a [base](base%20(chemistry).md) [precipitates](precipitate.md) out white lead(II) hydroxide}@}. Excess {@{strong base redissolves the precipitate and forms colorless tetrahydroxoplumbate(II) ions}@}: <!--SR:!2026-08-04,329,290!2026-09-19,483,250-->

> treating with [bases](base%20(chemistry).md)
>
> 1. {@{Pb<sup>2+</sup>(aq) + 2OH<sup>-</sup>(aq) → Pb(OH)<sub>2</sub>(s)}@}
> 2. {@{Pb(OH)<sub>2</sub>(s) + 2OH<sup>-</sup>(aq) → \[Pb(OH)<sub>4</sub>\]<sup>2-</sup>(aq)}@} <!--SR:!2026-11-02,938,330!2026-04-29,857,330-->
