# corrosive substance

A __corrosive substance__ is {@{a [substance](chemical%20substance.md) that damages other substances in contact by a [chemical reaction](chemical%20reaction.md)}@}. <!--SR:!fsrs,2032-03-02T05:57:33.382Z,2088,2088.45762649,3.98153807,2,11,0,0,2026-06-14T05:57:33.382Z-->

## protection

Use {@{[personal protective equipment](personal%20protective%20equipment.md), such as [gloves](glove.md), [safety spectacles](goggles.md), etc.}@} when handling corrosive substances. Perform experiements in {@{[fume cupboards](fume%20hood.md) to avoid breathing in corrosive [vapors](vapor.md)}@}. When diluting [acids](acid.md) or [bases](base%20(chemistry).md), {@{add them slowly to a large amount of [water](water.md) with stirring, but not in reverse, to avoid heating up the [liquids](liquid.md) and splattering them}@}. <!--SR:!2032-05-25,2535,330!2027-09-17,1059,270!2029-02-22,1399,270-->
