# solar granule

In [solar physics](solar%20physics.md) and [observation](solar%20observation.md), __granules__ are {@{[convection cells](convection%20cell.md) in the [Sun](Sun.md)'s [photosphere](photosphere.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2025-08-23,304,330-->

A typical granule has {@{a [diameter](diameter.md) on the order of 1&nbsp;500&nbsp;[kilometres](kilometre.md) (930&nbsp;mi), an area of about 700&nbsp;000&nbsp;km<sup>2</sup>}@}, and lasts {@{8 to 20 minutes before dissipating}@}. <!--SR:!2025-12-06,332,290!2025-02-02,124,270-->
