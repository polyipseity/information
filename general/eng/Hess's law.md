# Hess's law

__Hess's law of constant heat summation__, or more simply, __Hess's law__, {@{states that overall [enthalpy](enthalpy.md) change of a [chemical reaction](chemical%20reaction.md) is independent of the route taken if the initial and final states are the same}@}. <!--SR:!2032-09-08,2625,330-->
