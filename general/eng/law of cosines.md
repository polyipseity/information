# law of cosines

> __law of cosines__
>
> {@{$$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2 a b \cos \gamma$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$a, b, c$}@} are {@{the sides of a [triangle](triangle.md)}@}
> - {@{$\gamma$}@} is {@{the angle opposite to side $c$}@} <!--SR:!2026-02-16,545,310!2027-10-31,1094,350!2025-12-08,17,308!2025-12-07,16,308!2026-02-09,65,328-->
