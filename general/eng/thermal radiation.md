# thermal radiation

__Thermal radiation__ is {@{[electromagnetic radiation](electromagnetic%20radiation.md) emitted by the [thermal motion](kinetic%20theory%20of%20gases.md) of [particles](particle.md) in [matter](matter.md)}@}. {@{All matter with a [temperature](temperature.md) greater than [absolute zero](absolute%20zero.md) (0&nbsp;K = -273.15&nbsp;°C = -459.67&nbsp;°F)}@} emits thermal radiation. <!--SR:!2026-10-04,612,330!2029-04-16,1266,310-->
