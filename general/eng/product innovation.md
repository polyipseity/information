# product innovation

## classification of innovation

Product innovation can be {@{an invention or an improvement over existing products}@}. Making an invention is {@{costly, risky, and requires extensive research}@}. {@{95% of new inventions}@} fail. In contrast, improving existing products is {@{cheaper, safer, and requires limited research}@}. {@{98% of innovations}@} are of this kind. <!--SR:!2029-05-24,1411,330!2027-05-07,832,330!2027-12-08,931,290!2030-12-15,1845,330!2030-05-04,1673,330-->

Improving existing products can include {@{adding or modifying features, to align with current culture, market desirability, or trends}@}. <!--SR:!2028-08-31,1146,290-->
