# sine wave

> __sine wave as a function of both space and time__
>
> {@{$$y_\pm(x, t) = A \sin(kx \pm \omega t + \phi) + D$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - [function](function%20(mathematics).md) properties: {@{$y$}@} is {@{[displacement](displacement%20(geometry).md) at [position](position%20(geometry).md) $x$ and [time](time.md) $t$}@}, and for {@{$\pm$}@}, {@{positive is moving left and negative is moving right}@}
> - [wave](wave.md) properties: {@{$A$}@} is {@{[amplitude](amplitude.md)}@}, {@{$k$}@} is {@{[angular wavenumber](wavenumber.md)}@}, {@{$\omega$}@} is {@{[angular frequency](angular%20frequency.md)}@}, {@{$\phi$}@} is {@{phase offset}@}, and {@{$D$}@} is {@{equilibrium offset}@} <!--SR:!2026-09-15,746,334!2029-11-06,1607,334!2027-08-27,884,294!2026-02-19,74,343!2026-03-02,83,343!2026-02-18,73,343!2026-02-24,78,343!2026-03-02,83,343!2027-01-27,344,363!2026-03-02,83,343!2026-02-24,78,343!2026-02-25,79,343!2026-03-02,83,343!2027-01-16,334,363!2026-02-25,79,343-->
