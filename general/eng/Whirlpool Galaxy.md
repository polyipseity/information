# Whirlpool Galaxy

The {@{__Whirlpool Galaxy__}@}, also known as {@{__Messier 51a__ (__M51a__) or __NGC 5194__}@}, is {@{an [interacting](interacting%20galaxy.md) [grand-design](grand%20design%20spiral%20galaxy.md) [spiral galaxy](spiral%20galaxy.md) with a [Seyfert 2](seyfert%20galaxy.md) [active galactic nucleus](active%20galactic%20nucleus.md)}@}. It lies in {@{the [constellation](constellation.md) [Canes Venatici](Canes%20Venatici.md)}@}, and was the first galaxy to {@{be classified as a spiral galaxy}@}.
