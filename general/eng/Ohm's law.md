# Ohm's law

> __Ohm's law__
>
> {@{$$V = IR \text{ or } I = \frac V R \text{ or } R = \frac V I$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$V$}@} is {@{the [voltage](voltage.md)}@}
> - {@{$I$}@} is {@{the [current](electric%20current.md)}@}
> - {@{$R$}@} is {@{the [resistance](electrical%20resistance%20and%20conductance.md)}@} <!--SR:!2026-07-24,673,330!2026-08-10,685,330!2025-11-12,4,288!2025-11-12,4,288!2025-11-12,4,288!2025-11-12,4,288!2025-11-12,4,288-->
