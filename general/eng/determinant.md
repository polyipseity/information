# determinant

## two by two matrices

The determinant of {@{a 2 × 2 matrix $\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$}@} is denoted by {@{either "$\det$" or vertical bars surrounding the matrix values}@}, and is defined as {@{$$\det \begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix} = \begin{vmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{vmatrix} = ad - bc$$}@}. <!--SR:!2029-05-26,1404,350!2029-01-13,1301,350!2029-11-10,1502,330-->

## definition

### Leibniz formula

The _Leibniz formula_ for the determinant of a 3 × 3 matrix is: {@{$$\begin{vmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{vmatrix} = aei + bfg + cdh - ceg - bdi - afh$$}@}. <!--SR:!2028-07-17,1160,350-->
