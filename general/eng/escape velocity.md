# escape velocity

> __escape velocity__
>
> {@{$$v_e = \sqrt{\frac{2GM}r} = \sqrt{2gr}$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$v_e$}@} is {@{the escape velocity at distance $r$ from the center of a spherically symmetric primary body}@} of {@{[mass](mass.md) $M$}@}
> - {@{$g=\frac{GM}{r^2}$}@} is {@{the [gravitational acceleration](gravitational%20acceleration.md) at said distance}@}
> - {@{$G = 6.674\,30(15) \times 10^{−11} \mathrm{\ m^3 \cdot kg^{-1} \cdot s^{−2} }$}@} is {@{the [gravitational constant](gravitational%20constant.md)}@} <!--SR:!2027-10-27,1033,310!2028-03-13,1205,350!2026-03-19,431,210!2025-12-08,17,302!2025-12-08,17,302!2025-12-06,15,302!2025-12-07,16,302!2025-12-07,16,302-->
