# perpendicular axis theorem

> __perpendicular axis theorem__
>
> - condition: {@{the object}@} is {@{a [planar lamina](planar%20lamina.md), i.e. 2D body}@}, with {@{its body lying in the $xy$ plane}@}
>
> {@{$$I_z = I_x + I_y$$}@}
>
> where
>
> - {@{$I_x$, $I_y$, and $I_z$}@} are {@{[moments of inertia](moment%20of%20inertia.md) of an object about the axis $x$, $y$, and $z$}@}, which {@{meet at the origin, respectively}@} <!--SR:!2028-04-03,1219,350!fsrs,2033-12-14T00:00:00.000Z,2728,2728.00460833,1,2,10,0,0,2026-06-26T00:00:00.000Z!2027-02-08,351,350!2027-02-09,352,350!2027-01-10,328,350!2027-01-11,328,350!2026-12-13,305,350-->
