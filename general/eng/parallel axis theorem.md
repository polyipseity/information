# parallel axis theorem

## mass moment of inertia

> __parallel axis theorem for mass moment of inertia__
>
> {@{$$I = I_\mathrm{cm} + md^2$$}@}
>
> - where
>   - {@{$I$ is the [moment of inertia](moment%20of%20inertia.md) with respect to an axis that is parallel to and $d$ distance away from an axis that goes through the [center of mass](center%20of%20mass.md), which has a [moment of inertia](moment%20of%20inertia.md) of $I_\mathrm{cm}$}@} <!--SR:!2025-10-06,491,310!2027-02-21,833,330-->
