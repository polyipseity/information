# latitude

In [geography](geography.md), {@{__latitude__}@} is {@{a [coordinate](geographic%20coordinate%20systm.md) that specifies the [north](north.md)–[south](south.md) position of a point on the surface of the [Earth](Earth.md) or another [celestial body](astronomical%20object.md)}@}. Latitude is given as {@{an [angle](angle.md) that ranges from −90° at the [south pole](South%20Pole.md) to 90° at the [north pole](North%20Pole.md), with 0° at the [equator](equator.md)}@}.
