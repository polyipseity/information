# constellation

A __constellation__ is {@{an area on the [celestial sphere](celestial%20sphere.md) in which a group of visible [stars](star.md) forms [a perceived pattern](asterism%20(astronomy).md) or outline}@}, typically {@{representing an animal, mythological subject, or inanimate object}@}.
