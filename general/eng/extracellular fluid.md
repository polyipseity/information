# extracellular fluid

{@{__Extracellular fluid__ (__ECF__)}@} is {@{all [body fluid](body%20fluid.md) outside the cells of a [multicellular organism](multicellular%20organism.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2027-11-29,1175,290!2026-02-09,16,294-->

## components

### interstitial fluid

{@{Interstitial fluid or tissue fluid}@} provides {@{a constant environment for cells, a medium to exchange cellular materials, and forms [lymph](lymph.md)}@}. <!--SR:!2029-12-28,1443,250!2026-02-08,15,294-->
