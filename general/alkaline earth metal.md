# alkaline earth metal

The __alkaline earth metals__ {{consist of the [chemical elements](chemical%20element.md) [beryllium](beryllium.md) (Be), [magnesium](magnesium.md) (Mg), [calcium](calcium.md) (Ca), [strontium](strontium.md) (Sr), [barium](barium.md) (Ba), and [radium](radium.md) (Ra)}}. The alkaline earth metals are {{all relatively-low-[density](density.md), and somewhat [reactive](reactivity%20(chemistry).md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-10-30,82,190!2023-10-24,121,230-->
