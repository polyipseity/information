__Copernican heliocentrism__ is {{the astronomical [model](scientific%20modelling.md) developed by [Nicolaus Copernicus](Nicolaus%20Copernicus.md)}} and published in {{1543}}. This model {{positioned the [Sun](Sun.md) at the center of the [universe](universe.md), motionless, with [Earth](Earth.md) and the other [planets](planet.md) moving around it in [circular orbits](circular%20orbit.md), modified by [epicycles](deferent%20and%20epicycle.md), and at uniform speeds}}. The Copernican model displaced {{the [Ptolemaic model](geocentric%20model.md#Ptolemaic%20model) that had prevailed for centuries, which had placed Earth at the center of the universe}}. <!--SR:!2024-07-11,11,270!2024-07-14,7,230!2024-07-12,13,270!2024-07-10,11,270-->
