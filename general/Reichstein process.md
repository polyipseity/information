## importance

In the modern two-stage fermentation process, {{the protection and oxidation steps are replaced by another [genetically modified](genetic%20engineering.md) bacteria oxidizing [sorbose](sorbose.md) to 2-ketogluonic acid directly}}. The production cost is {{two-third of the Reichstein process}}.
