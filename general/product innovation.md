# product innovation

## classification of innovation

Product innovation can be {{an invention or an improvement over existing products}}. Making an invention is {{costly, risky, and requires extensive research}}. {{95% of new inventions}} fail. In contrast, improving existing products is {{cheaper, safer, and requires limited research}}. {{98% of innovations}} are of this kind. <!--SR:!2024-08-18,82,290!2024-07-15,63,310!2024-09-13,91,270!2024-09-21,107,290!2024-09-08,97,290-->

Improving existing products can include {{adding or modifying features, to align with current culture, market desirability, or trends}}. <!--SR:!2024-09-06,87,250-->
