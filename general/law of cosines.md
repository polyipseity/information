# law of cosines

> __law of cosines__
>
> {{$$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2 a b \cos \gamma$$}}
>
> - where
>   - {{$a, b, c$ are the sides of a [triangle](triangle.md), and $\gamma$ is the angle opposite to side $c$}} <!--SR:!2024-08-20,176,310!2024-11-01,236,330-->
