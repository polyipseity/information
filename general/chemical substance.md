# chemical substance

A __chemical substance__ is {{a matter with characteristic properties and constant [chemical composition](chemical%20composition.md)}}. It can either be {{a [chemical element](#chemical%20element) or a [chemical compound](#chemical%20compound)}}.<sup>[1](#^note-1)</sup> It is pure, {{while impure ones are [mixtures](mixture.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-12-28,358,230!2024-09-07,382,290!2024-05-25,328,330-->

## properties

Compare with [mixture ยง properties](mixture.md#properties).

- composition ::: fixed, definite, constant <!--SR:!2024-09-06,381,290!2024-03-12,4,298-->
- formation ::: [chemical reaction](chemical%20reaction.md) occurs <!--SR:!2024-03-28,283,330!2024-03-12,4,298-->
- properties ::: different from its constituents <!--SR:!2024-09-19,370,290!2024-03-12,4,298-->
- separation ::: separable by chemical methods but not physical methods <!--SR:!2026-01-01,769,330!2024-03-12,4,298-->
- [boiling point](boiling%20point.md) and [melting point](melting%20point.md) ::: sharp <!--SR:!2026-02-14,806,330!2024-03-12,4,298-->

## chemical element

An element cannot be {{broken down or transformed by chemical methods}}. <!--SR:!2026-04-11,783,290-->

## chemical compound

A compound is made up of {{multiple elements chemically combined}}. <!--SR:!2025-05-15,553,290-->
