# critical point (mathematics)

For [functions of a real variable](function%20of%20a%20real%20variable.md), a __critical point__ {{is a [point](point%20(geometry).md) in the domain of the function at which the [derivative](derivative.md) does not exist or equals zero}}. <!--SR:!2024-05-06,78,290-->
