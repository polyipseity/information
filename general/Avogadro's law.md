# Avogadro's law

__{{Avogadro's}} law__ states that {{equal volumes of all [gases](gas.md) at the same [temperature](temperature.md) and [pressure](pressure.md) contain the same number of [molecules](molecule.md)}}. It is a specific case of {{the [ideal gas law](ideal%20gas%20law.md)}}.
