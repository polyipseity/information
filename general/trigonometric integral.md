# trigonometric integral

## sine integral

> {{__Si(x), si(x)__}}
>
> - {{$\operatorname{Si}(x)=\int_0^x\!\frac{\sin{t}}t\,\mathrm{d}t$}}
> - {{$\operatorname{si}(x)=-\int_x^\infty\!\frac{\sin{t}}t\,\mathrm{d}t=\operatorname{Si}(x)-\frac\pi2$}} <!--SR:!2023-10-04,4,270!2023-10-01,1,230!2023-10-01,1,230-->

## cosine integral

> {{__Cin(x), Ci(x)__}}
>
> - {{$\operatorname{Cin}(x)=\int_0^x\!\frac{1-\cos{t}}t\,\mathrm{d}t$}}
> - {{$\operatorname{Ci}(x)=-\int_x^\infty\!\frac{\cos{t}}t\,\mathrm{d}t=\gamma+\ln{x}-\operatorname{Cin}(x)$}}
> - where
>   - $\gamma$ is the [Euler's constant](Euler's%20constant.md) <!--SR:!2023-10-04,4,270!2023-10-01,1,230!2023-10-01,1,230-->
