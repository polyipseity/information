# stellar nucleosynthesis

In [astrophysics](astrophysics.md), __stellar nucleosynthesis__ is {{the [creation](nucleosynthesis.md) of [chemical elements](chemical%20element.md) by [nuclear fusion](nuclear%20fusion.md) reactions within [stars](star.md)}}. Stellar nucleosynthesis has occurred since {{the [original creation](Big%20Bang%20nucleosynthesis.md) of [hydrogen](hydrogen.md), [helium](helium.md) and [lithium](lithium.md) during the [Big Bang](Big%20Bang.md)}}. <!--SR:!2024-10-23,65,310!2024-09-30,43,290-->
