# Ohm's law

> __Ohm's law__
>
> {{$$V = IR \text{ or } I = \frac V R \text{ or } R = \frac V I$$}}
>
> - where
>   - {{$V$ is the [voltage](voltage.md), $I$ is the [current](electric%20current.md), and $R$ is the [resistance](electrical%20resistance%20and%20conductance.md)}}
