# equilibrium constant

The __equilibrium constant__ ($K_c$) {{of a [chemical equation](chemical%20equation.md) is the value of its [reaction quotient](reaction%20quotient.md) at [chemical equilibrium](chemical%20equilibrium.md)}}. It is dependent on {{[temperature](temperature.md), [solvent](solvent.md), [ionic strength](ionic%20strength.md), etc.}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270!2023-04-09,4,274-->

When determining the equilibrium constant, {{constant [concentrations](concentration.md) (e.g. concentrations of [liquids](liquid.md) and [solids](solid.md)) can be incorporated into the constant for simplicity}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270-->
