# ester

## physical properties

### boiling and melting points

Due to increased [polarizability](polarizability.md), esters have {{[boiling points](boiling%20point.md) and [melting points](melting%20point.md) in between comparable [alkanes](alkane.md) and [alcohols](alcohol.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270-->

### density

Esters are {{less [dense](density.md) than [water](water.md)}}. Density {{decrease}} with increasing size. <!--SR:!2023-04-05,1,230!2023-04-05,1,230-->

### odor

Most esters have {{a pleasant fruity smell}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270-->

### solubility

Esters {{that are small are [soluble](solubility.md) in [water](water.md)}}. Solubility {{decreases}} the larger the ester. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270!2023-04-08,4,270-->
