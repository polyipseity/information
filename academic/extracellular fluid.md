# extracellular fluid

__Extracellular fluid__ (__ECF__) is {{all [body fluid](body%20fluid.md) outside the cells of a [multicellular organism](multicellular%20organism.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-08,3,250-->

## components

### interstitial fluid

Interstitial fluid or tissue fluid provides {{a constant environment for cells, a medium to exchange cellular materials, and forms [lymph](lymph.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-08,3,250-->
