# electron excitation

__Electron excitation__ (__electron relaxation__) is {{the transfer of a bound [electron](electron.md) to a more (less) energetic bound state}}. It can occur via {{[photoexcitation](photoexcitation.md) (PE)}}, where {{the electron absorbs a [photon](photon.md) and gain all its energy}}, or {{collisional excitation (CE)}}, where {{the electron gains partial energy from colliding with another energetic electron}}. <!--SR:!2023-06-23,103,270!2023-10-27,218,310!2023-04-08,55,210!2023-04-13,35,290!2023-08-27,160,270-->

When energy gain {{is above ionization energy for the above processes}}, {{[ionization](ionization.md) occurs instead}}. <!--SR:!2023-07-17,151,290!2023-04-16,76,270-->
