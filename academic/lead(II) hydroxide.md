# lead(II) hydroxide

__Lead(II) hydroxide__ is {{an [inorganic compound](inorganic%20compound.md) with the [chemical formula](chemical%20formula.md) [Pb](lead.md)([OH](hydroxide.md))<sub>2</sub>}}. It looks like {{white amorphous [powder](powder.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-19,13,290!2023-04-07,2,230-->

## preparation

Treating {{lead(II) [salts](salt%20(chemistry).md) with a [base](base%20(chemistry).md) [precipitates](precipitate.md) out white lead(II) hydroxide}}. Excess {{strong base redissolves the precipitate and forms colorless tetrahydroxoplumbate(II) ions}}: <!--SR:!2023-04-23,17,290!2023-04-12,6,250-->

> treating with [bases](base%20(chemistry).md)
> 1. {{Pb<sup>2+</sup>(aq) + 2OH<sup>-</sup>(aq) → Pb(OH)<sub>2</sub>(s)}}
> 2. {{Pb(OH)<sub>2</sub>(s) + 2OH<sup>-</sup>(aq) → \[Pb(OH)<sub>4</sub>\]<sup>2-</sup>(aq)}} <!--SR:!2023-04-22,16,290!2023-04-18,12,270-->
