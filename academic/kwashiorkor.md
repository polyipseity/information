# kwashiorkor

__Kwashiorkor__ is {{a form of severe [protein–energy malnutrition](protein–energy%20malnutrition.md)}}. It is characterised by {{[edema](edema.md) like a swollen [abdomen](abdomen.md) caused by accumulation of [tissue fluid](extracellular%20fluid.md) and weak [muscles](muscle.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-22,13,270!2023-06-12,26,250-->
