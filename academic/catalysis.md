# catalysis

__Catalysis__ is {{the process of increasing the [reaction rate](reaction%20rate.md) of a [chemical reaction](chemical%20reaction.md) by adding a [substance](chemical%20substance.md) called a __[catalyst](catalysis.md)__}}. Catalysts are {{not consumed in the reaction and remain chemically unchanged afterwards}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,3,250!2023-04-08,4,270-->

## significance

Catalysts can {{increase production throughput, reducing production cost and save [fuels](fuel.md)}}. In [biology](biology.md), {{[enzymes](enzyme.md) are [protein](protein.md)-based catalysts}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,4,270!2023-04-08,3,250-->
