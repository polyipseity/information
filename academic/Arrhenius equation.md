# Arrhenius equation

The {{__Arrhenius equation__}} is {{a formula for the dependence of [rate constant](reaction%20rate%20constant.md) on [temperature](temperature.md)}}.

## equation

The Arrhenius equation is given as

> {{$$k=Ae^\frac{-E_a}{RT}.$$}}
