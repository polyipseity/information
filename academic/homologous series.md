# homologous series

A __homologous series__ is {{a [series](sequence.md) of [chemical compounds](chemical%20compound.md) with the same [functional group](functional%20group.md), similar [chemical properties](chemical%20property.md), and gradual changes in [physical properties](physical%20property.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-07-01,24,230-->

Examples include {{[alkanes](alkane.md) and [alkenes](alkene.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-07-01,69,310-->
