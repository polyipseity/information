# chemical formula

## types

### empirical formula

Empirical formulas {{show the simplest whole number ratio of [atoms](atom.md) in a [chemical compound](chemical%20compound.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-08,27,270-->

### molecular formula

Molecular formulas {{show the number of each kind of [atom](atom.md) in a [molecule](molecule.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-21,38,270-->

### structural formula

Structural formulas {{show the numbers, organization and [chemical bonds](chemical%20bond.md) of [atoms](atom.md) in a [molecule](molecule.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-14,13,230-->

#### condensed formula

Condensed formulas {{can represent simple structures in a line of text}}. [Multiple bonds](bond%20order.md) are {{generally explicitly shown}}. <!--SR:!2023-07-11,74,270!2023-06-05,54,295-->

#### skeletal formula

- See: [skeletal formula](skeletal%20formula.md)

Skeletal formulas {{can represent more complex [organic compounds](organic%20compound.md)}}. In a skeletal formula, {{[carbon](carbon.md) atoms are represented by [vertices](vertx%20(geometry).md) and [hydrogen](hydrogen.md) atoms attached to carbon atoms are omitted}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-20,37,270!2023-05-08,29,255-->
