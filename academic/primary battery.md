# primary battery

A __primary battery__ or __primary cell__ is {{an [electric battery](electric%20battery.md) that is designed to be discharged once and then [disposed](disposable%20product.md)}}, unlike a [secondary battery](rechargeable%20battery.md). <!--SR:!2023-08-28,97,270-->
