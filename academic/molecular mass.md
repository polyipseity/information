# molecular mass

The __molecular mass__ is {{the [mass](mass.md) of a given [molecule](molecule.md)}}. The related __relative molecular mass__ is {{the ratio of the mass of the molecule to the [unified atomic mass unit](dalton%20(unit).md) and is unitless}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-15,10,270!2023-04-16,11,270-->
