# boiling chip

A __boiling chip__ is {{a tiny, rough piece of material added to [liquids](liquid.md) to smoothen [boiling](boiling.md)}}. It prevents {{[bumping](bumping%20(chemistry).md) due to [superheating](superheating.md) of liquid}}. <!--SR:!2023-03-30,1,210!2023-03-30,3,250-->
