# dalton

The __dalton__ or __unified atomic mass unit__ {{(symbols: __Da__ or __u__)}} is {{a non-SI unit of [mass](mass.md) defined as $\frac1{12}$ of the mass of an [unbound](chemical%20bond.md) neutral [atom](atom.md) of [carbon-12](carbon-12.md) in its electronic and nuclear [ground state](ground%20state.md) and [at rest](invariant%20mass.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-08-17,93,270!2023-06-10,36,208-->
