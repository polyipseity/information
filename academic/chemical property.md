# chemical property

A __chemical property__ is {{a [property](property.md) that can be measured only by [chemical reactions](chemical%20reaction.md)}}. It is contrasted with {{[physical property](physical%20property.md)}}. It describes {{reaction with other [chemical substances](chemical%20substance.md) or [chemical change](chemical%20change.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-08,9,250!2023-03-31,4,270!2023-04-06,7,252-->
