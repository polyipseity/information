# ferroxyl indicator solution

__Ferroxyl indicator solution__ {{consists of [potassium hexacyanoferrate(III)](potassium%20ferricyanide.md) and [phenolphthalein](phenolphthalein.md)}}. The former turns {{blue in the presence of [iron](iron.md)(II) ions}}. The latter turns {{pink in the presence of [hydroxide ions](hydroxide.md)}}. <!--SR:!2023-05-11,26,250!2023-05-16,31,270!2023-06-14,53,290-->
