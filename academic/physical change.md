# physical change

__Physical changes__ are {{changes affecting the form of [chemical substances](chemical%20substance.md)}}. They contrast with [chemical changes](chemical%20change.md). <!--SR:!2023-04-17,9,250-->

In a physical change, substances {{undergo a change without affecting their [chemical composition](chemical%20composition.md)}}. No {{new substances are formed}}. <!--SR:!2023-04-16,7,250!2023-04-24,17,290-->
