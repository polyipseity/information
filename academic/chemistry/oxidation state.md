# oxidation state

## simple approach
> 1. ==element in a free form: 0==
> 2. ==ionic compound or ion: charge==
> 3. ==fluorine: -1==
> 4. ==(if not bonded to a lighter halogen, oxygen, or nitrogen) chlorine, bromine: -1==
> 5. ==group I metals: +1==
> 6. ==group II metals: +2==
> 7. ==metallic hydride: -1==
> 8. ==hydrogen: +1==
