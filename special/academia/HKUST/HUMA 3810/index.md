# index

- HKUST HUMA 3810
- name: Taoism and the Chinese Tradition
- credits: 3

---

This undergraduate humanities course introduces Taoism as a philosophy of life, with special emphasis on Lao Tzu, Chuang Tzu, and close reading of their primary texts. The course root keeps logistics and navigation in English, while topic-specific notes are written mainly in Traditional Chinese.

The content is in teaching order.
