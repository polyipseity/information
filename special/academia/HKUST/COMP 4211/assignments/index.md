# index

- HKUST COMP 4211 assignments

---

This folder collects assignment notes for `COMP 4211`. The course has 4 written assignments and a larger menu of hands-on assignments, from which students must choose exactly 6 submissions for grading. Additional hands-on submissions are not graded.

Generative artificial intelligence tools are allowed for written assignments only when their use is disclosed clearly, including enough prompt detail to explain how the tool was used. AI-generated code is prohibited in hands-on assignments.

Canvas submissions receive Turnitin similarity scores unless stated otherwise. Scores above 40 trigger a penalty of $(\max\{\text{similarity score}-40, 0\}/60)\times 100\%$ of the item grade. Students are advised to keep a timestamped screenshot of the final submitted similarity score.

Late submissions are accepted for up to 5 days after the due date with a 5% penalty per day. The course materials, assignments, examinations, and solutions are copyright-protected and should not be shared outside the enrolled cohort.
