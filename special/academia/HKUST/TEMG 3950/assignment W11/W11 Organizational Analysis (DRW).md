# Organizational Analysis (DRW)

This is an individual assignment. Past assignment example is provided here to level the playing field.  You are NOT allowed to approach other students for theirs past assignment, and penalty will be imposed if you do so - to both students.  <span style="color: #e03e2d;">Similarity % reported by TurnItIn will be subtracted from the final score.</span>

Case paper: [W11 DRW 9-916-535.PDF](https://canvas.ust.hk/courses/56300/files/8864778?wrap=1)
Past assignment: [W11 DRW past example.pdf](https://canvas.ust.hk/courses/56300/files/8748403?wrap=1)

DRW Technologies is facing reduction in profitability from both customers segments of US defense sector and commercial airlines manufacturers.  A new <span style="color: #e03e2d;">position of corporate VP of Procurement was created to reduce spending and do so quickly. Ed Claiborne was hired for the job, and he rolled out a new policy to meet his goal of cost reduction</span>.  Nothing happened.

Please conduct situation analysis of the reactions to Claiborne's new procurement policies:

1. <u>Root Cause Analysis in Cause Mapping notation </u>(<u>2-3 slides</u>): key business issue(s) on the LHS, and root causes on the RHS. Incorporate dimensions from McKinsey's 7S framework so that your exploration of underlying causes is comprehensive. You should have >5Why's on each of the 7 dimensions, >7 "evidence boxes“ using facts from the case or your own imagination and >5 “solution boxes” to support steps in the RCA.  Attached is an imperfect example from the previous class to get you started but it many things may be inaccurate.  You can place 7 dimensions on no more than 3 slides. Somewhere on the slide, give a report card of your assessment of DRW's organizational capabilities by 7S dimensions: Red dot for "broken", Yellow dot for "slightly-broken" or Green dot for "OK".
2. <u>A table of solutions </u>(<u>1 slide</u>): describe your creative best 5 solutions to turn the situation around. Your "solutions" table should be based on information from the case, and you can supplement information using logical deduction and creativity with column headers of "No.", "Description", "Who", "Weeks" and "Budget". (e.g., "No." is 1 to <span style="color: #e03e2d;">5+</span>; "Description" is a short solution description; "Who" is the title of person who would be responsible for this solution strategy; and “Weeks" is your estimation of how many weeks it will take to develop the solution to pilot test complete stage, and "Budget" is your estimation of costs in USD.)
3. <u>An email in 5-Step Persuasive Selling format </u>(<u>1 slide</u>): your name is Bruce Lee, an external management consultant hired by the CEO to turn around the situation. Your email should be 200-250 words in >= 8 pt. font size.

Please submit a .pdf or .pptx file in landscape orientation.
