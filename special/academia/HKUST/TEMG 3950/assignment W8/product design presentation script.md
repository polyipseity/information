# TEMG 3950 product design presentation script

## pricing model

- two components
  - care watch, one-time cost $900
  - subscription, requires a care watch, monthly $250
    - optional emergency, e.g. for Uber, current usage, NOT revenue
- printer/ink model
  - profit margin of the watch is low initially
  - most profit is made on the subscription
- price is on lower end
  - competitor product range cheaper $100~$2000, not explain why cheaper yet

## market size

- TAM 4.29 billion
  - elderly suffering from early-stage or mild cognitive impairment in GBA
  - late-stage impairment not considered because they need more specialized care watch
- SAM 1.04 billion
  - GBA to Hong Kong because we launch our product on Hong Kong first, GBA later
- SOM 104 million
  - 10% of the market share
