# Blue Ocean Strategy (PetWellClinic)

This an individual assignment.

Because case paper on PetWellClinic is 17 pages plus it has answers within, I've summarized the case in the class meeting handout, [W12 BOS and PetWellClinic Handout (2024.04.23).pdf](https://canvas.ust.hk/courses/56300/files/8956151?wrap=1)

Please watch the following 2-minute online videos multiple times by Dr. Sam Meisler and Dr. Kathryn Comeford from PetWellClinic for insight on what are important to multiple stakeholders, namely pet owners, vets and clinic owners.  (Sorry, pets. You would be a stakeholder too if you could talk...)

1. [Being A Vet Is Hard (blueoceanstrategy.com)](https://teach.blueoceanstrategy.com/petwell1/)
2. [Eliminate, Reduce, Raise, Create (blueoceanstrategy.com)](https://teach.blueoceanstrategy.com/petwell2)
3. [Happy Vets, Healthy Pets (blueoceanstrategy.com)](https://teach.blueoceanstrategy.com/petwell5)

Please use the Blue Ocean Strategy Worksheet template for analysis.  <span style="color: #e03e2d;">Instructions for each worksheet are provided in the "speaker notes" section below the slide.</span>  Assignment template: [W12 PetWellClinic Assignment Template (2024.04.24).pptx](https://canvas.ust.hk/courses/56300/files/8959508?wrap=1)

1. Capture "As-is Customer Journey Map" for Veterinarians.
    - Modify stages in a Vet's "day in life" according to your own understanding.
    - Add tasks that this person needs to do under "Jobs"; try to be comprehensive covering as many roles as possible.
    - Add pains
    - Add gains
2. Capture "As-is Customer Journey Map" for Pet Owners.
3. Capture "Non-customers" - describe the 4 types of customers by filling in the box 1 sentence for Vet/Pet Owner/Clinic Owner, if any.  Remember to use the definition of each tier according to the definition on the slide.
4. ERRC strategies for the new "PetWellClinic" (assuming it doesn't exist yet) with >15 actions total with ~3 items for each of the 4 boxes.  
5. "To-be" Strategy Canvas with "Traditional Pet Clinic" and "PetWellClinic" when Dr. Sam Meisler entered the market with this new service. Instruction: (1) use “edit data” on the chart to enter 3 new attributes, (2) add your assessment of each dimension for PetWellClinic, and (3) move 4 “bars” to indicate beginning of “eliminate”, “reduce”, “raise” and “create” actions. Note: price is NOT included in the 4 ERRC sections because you cannot eliminate price.
6. 6-Path Strategies. Instructions: pick 4 strategies and place a short sentence to summarize what Dr. Sam Meisler did when creating PetWellClinic, from the perspective of Vets, pet owners, and others in the ecosystem.  Describe the strategy using 2-4 sentences in the table.

Note: submit assignment in pdf format by converting PPTX to pdf in landscape mode. Double-check that pdf prints correctly.  Also, if you need to adjust the template to improve it, feel free to do so.
