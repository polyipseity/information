## format

- header ::@:: company name, `(Consolidated) Statement of Cash Flows`, duration, (monetary unit) <!--SR:!2024-12-28,15,290!2024-12-28,15,290-->
  - header / duration ::@:: example: `For the Year ended December 31, 2019`, `For the period September 1, 2024 to December 13, 2015` <!--SR:!2024-12-27,14,290!2024-12-29,16,290-->
  - header / monetary unit ::@:: example: <br/> (none; not recommended) <br/> `(in million of dollars, except per share data)` <!--SR:!2024-12-27,14,290!2024-12-28,15,290-->
- dollar sign ::@:: Add it before the first number ("net income") and the last numbers ("cash & cash equivalents at end of period"). The rest can be omitted. <!--SR:!2024-12-29,16,290!2024-12-27,14,290-->
- total ::@:: Calculate "net cash provided by operating activities", "net cash provided by investing activities", "net cash provided by financing activities", and "cash & cash equivalents at end of period" from "cash & cash equivalents at beginning of period". <!--SR:!2024-12-28,15,290!2024-12-28,15,290-->
  - total / format ::@:: Add a (over)line above the total. <p> For "cash & cash equivalents at end of period", additionally add double (under)lines below the total. <!--SR:!2024-12-27,14,290!2024-12-28,15,290-->
- negative number ::@:: Write negative numbers in brackets, not with a minus sign. For example, write `(42)` instead of `-42`. <!--SR:!2024-12-27,14,290!2024-12-29,16,290-->
- casing ::@:: Use title case for basically everything!!! (Ignore that I have been NOT using title case in this note.) <!--SR:!2024-12-27,14,290!2024-12-27,14,290-->
- (root) ::@:: cash flows from operating activities (+) <br/> cash flows for investing activities (+) <br/> cash flows from financing activities (+) <br/> net increase (decrease) in cash & cash equivalents: sum above <br/> cash & cash equivalents at beginning of period <br/> cash & cash equivalents at end of period: sum appropriately <!--SR:!2025-01-20,29,270!2025-01-14,24,250-->
  - (root) / (+) ::@:: (+) means "has children". <!--SR:!2024-12-29,16,290!2024-12-27,14,290-->
  - (root) / cash flows from operating activities ::@:: net income <br/> adjustments to reconcile net income to net cash provided by operating activities (+) <br/> net cash provided by operating activities: sum above <!--SR:!2025-01-14,24,250!2025-01-20,29,270-->
    - (root) / cash flows from operating activities / adjustments to reconcile net income to net cash provided by operating activities ::@:: depreciation and amortization <br/> changes in assets and liabilities (+) <!--SR:!2024-12-29,16,290!2025-01-14,24,250-->
      - (root) / cash flows from operating activities / adjustments to reconcile net income to net cash provided by operating activities / changes in assets and liabilities ::@:: examples: <br/> accounts receivable <br/> inventory <br/> prepaid expenses <br/> accounts payable <br/> accrued expenses <!--SR:!2025-01-17,27,270!2024-12-29,16,290-->
  - (root) / cash flows for investing activities ::@:: examples: <br/> purchases of property, plant and equipment <br/> proceeds from disposal of property, plant and equipment <br/> purchase of short-term investments <br/> proceeds from short-term investments <br/> net cash used by investing activities: sum above <!--SR:!2025-01-14,24,250!2025-01-14,24,250-->
  - (root) / cash flows from financing activities ::@:: examples: <br/> purchase of treasury stock <br/> proceeds from issuance of stock <br/> net cash used in financing activities: sum above <!--SR:!2025-01-27,37,290!2025-01-19,26,270-->
- validation ::@:: "Cash & cash equivalents at end of period" should agree with the balance sheet. <!--SR:!2024-12-28,15,290!2024-12-29,16,290-->
