# lambda expressions

## lambda capture

If the capture-default is `&`, subsequent simple captures {@{must not begin with `&`}@}.

If the capture-default is `=`, subsequent simple captures {@{must begin with `&`. Since C++17, `*this` is allowed. Since C++20, `this` is also allowed}@}.
