### profile summary

Two recommended formats:

1. I–you ::: what I want from you; what I can do for you; requirements, if any <!--SR:!2024-06-11,166,310!2024-06-15,75,353-->
2. A.S.K. ::: attitude; skills; knowledge; requirements, if any <!--SR:!2024-11-07,299,330!2024-06-19,79,353-->

### experience

- industry experience ::: if no salary <!--SR:!2024-05-18,110,310!2024-07-01,87,353-->
- work experience ::: all, 0 to 3 sub-points according to importance <!--SR:!2024-08-19,234,330!2024-06-12,72,353-->
  - work experience sub-points ::: statistics (numbers); IROAR: impact, results, outcomes, achievements, responsibilities <!--SR:!2024-06-02,68,210!2024-06-26,82,353-->

### skills

The most important skills in general are {{adaptivity, fast learning, and resilience}}. <!--SR:!2024-04-23,127,290-->

## referees
