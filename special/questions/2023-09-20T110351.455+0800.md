# 2023-09-20T110351.455+0800

Let $M=\begin{bmatrix}1&p\\q&r\end{bmatrix}$ and $|M|=1$. Find $M^{-1}$.

## solution

$$\begin{aligned}
M^{-1}&=\frac1{|M|}\begin{bmatrix}r&-p\\-q&1\end{bmatrix}\\
&=\begin{bmatrix}r&-p\\-q&1\end{bmatrix}
\end{aligned}$$
